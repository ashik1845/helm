Register Your Emergency Contact

This emergency contact registration is part of an integrated motorcycle safety system. The system detects critical events such as severe impacts or sudden falls and automatically alerts the nearest hospital with the rider's location and impact data. Additionally, if the rider experiences an accident or any emergency situation, an alert will be sent to the emergency contact you provide below.

In case of an accident, your provided emergency contact will receive real-time notifications about the accident. They will be informed about the situation and your current location, ensuring timely action to ensure your safety.
